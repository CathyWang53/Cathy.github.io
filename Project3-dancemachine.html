Solutions

1. What are the requirments and solutions of each part?

Based on the overall effect of this dance machine, there will be four main parts to deal with feet, hands, heart rates and signals. Each part will have different key points.

 

2. How does each part work together?

User's hand gestures, foot steps and heart rates are captured distinctively. The signals are then transmitted into the computer. Unity codes will calculate the scores based on user's performances and display it on the screen. Also, it will adjust music speed based on user's heart rates.

3

Results

User can wear the pulse sensor on his/her finger, step on the buttons of the blanket according to the arrows and move their hand to catch the balls.

The music speed will be adjusted according to the heart rate. When the heart rate is higher than a threshold value, the speed will decrease and vice versa.

The scores will be shown on the screen. If users step on the right button, scores will plus ten and if users lose a ball, scores will decrease one.

 

 

Finally, the magic dance machine won the first place within 20 teams in the intelligent system competition.
